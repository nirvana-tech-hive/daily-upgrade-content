# 📜 Counter-AI Movement: Storyline Framework

## **The Looped Narrative**
Our content follows a **year-long "Hero’s Journey"** for learners, looping annually with increasing depth.

### **Narrative Phases**
1. **Awakening (Q1):** Learners realize the risks of AI dependency.
2. **Training (Q2):** They build skills to counteract AI reliance.
3. **Challenge (Q3):** They apply skills in real-world scenarios.
4. **Mastery (Q4):** They achieve independence and mentor others.

### **How It Works**
- Each **content arm** contributes to the narrative.
- **Weekly challenges** reinforce the story (e.g., "No-AI Week").
- **User progress** is tracked and celebrated.
