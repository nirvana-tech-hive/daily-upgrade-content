**The Problem**
Many brands and creators lack consistency in their visual identity, making their work look unprofessional and forgettable.

**The Exercise**
Today, create a **Brand Style Guide** in Canva:
1. Open Canva and start a new project.
2. Add your brand colors, fonts, and logo.
3. Create templates for social media posts, presentations, and documents.

**Why It Matters**
A strong brand style guide ensures **consistency** and **professionalism**. It’s a skill that **AI can’t replicate**—it requires human creativity and design sense.

**Counter-AI Connection**
AI tools like Canva’s AI features can generate designs, but **only you can define your brand’s unique identity**. Train your brain to think creatively and strategically.
