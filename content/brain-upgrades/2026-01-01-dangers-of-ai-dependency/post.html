Brain Upgrade #1: The Dangers of AI Dependency

The Problem

AI is making us lazy. We no longer memorize phone numbers, addresses, or even birthdays. Our brains are getting weaker.

The Exercise

Today, memorize 5 new pieces of information (e.g., a grocery list, a friend’s phone number). Use the Memory Palace Technique.

Why It Matters

A strong memory is your first defense against AI dependency. The more you rely on your brain, the sharper it becomes.

Counter-AI Connection

AI tools like Google and Siri are convenient, but convenience is the enemy of competence. Train your brain daily.
