**The Problem**
AI tools are powerful, but **misusing them can lead to dependency, bias, and ethical dilemmas**.

**The Exercise**
Today, explore **one ethical AI use case** (e.g., using AI for learning, not cheating).

**Why It Matters**
AI is a tool, not a crutch. Using it **ethically and responsibly** ensures you stay in control of your learning and creativity.

**Counter-AI Connection**
AI tools like ChatGPT can generate content, but **only you can ensure it’s accurate, ethical, and aligned with your goals**. Train your brain to use AI as a **cognitive amplifier**, not a replacement.
