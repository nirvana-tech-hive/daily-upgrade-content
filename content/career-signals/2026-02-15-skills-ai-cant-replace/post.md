**The Problem**
AI is automating repetitive tasks, but **human skills like creativity, empathy, and critical thinking** are becoming more valuable than ever.

**The Exercise**
Identify **one skill** that AI can’t replace (e.g., creativity, emotional intelligence, strategic thinking) and practice it daily.

**Why It Matters**
Jobs that require **human judgment, creativity, and emotional intelligence** are future-proof. These skills ensure you remain **irreplaceable** in the workforce.

**Counter-AI Connection**
AI tools can analyze data, but **only humans can innovate, empathize, and lead**. Train your brain to think critically and creatively.
